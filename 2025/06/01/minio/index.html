Minio permission

  • S3 Object Locking enables you to store objects on MinIO using the write-once-read-many (WORM) model. You can either attach a retention period or legal hold to objects.

    • The former prevents any overwrites until the retention period has expired, while the later disallows overwrites until the legal hold has been removed from the object.
